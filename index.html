Survival Tips

How to Survive in the World of the Walking Dead

  • Stay calm and avoid noise that might attract zombies.
  • Keep moving and don't stay in one place for too long.
  • Ensure multiple exits in any place you hide.
  • Gather essential supplies like food, water, and medicine.
  • Learn basic skills like first aid and hunting.
  • Be as cautious of other humans as you are of zombies.
  • Use melee weapons instead of firearms when possible to avoid drawing attention.
  • Practice close combat and how to target a zombie's brain.
